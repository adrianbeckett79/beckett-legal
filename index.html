What we do

Commercial law, no borders

We focus on the work that matters most when business crosses into Finland — or when Finnish business crosses out.

International Contracts

Cross-border commercial agreements, distribution deals, licensing, joint ventures. We handle governing law selection, dispute resolution design, and CISG considerations so your contracts actually work across jurisdictions.

Employment for Multinationals

Hiring Finnish employees? Finnish employment law is heavily employee-protective and non-negotiable. We draft compliant contracts, navigate collective agreements, and translate your global HR policies into Finnish reality.

M&A and Corporate

Acquisitions, investments, and corporate structuring with a Nordic and Baltic focus. Due diligence, transaction documents, and regulatory filings — coordinated with local counsel when the deal spans borders.

Dispute Resolution

Commercial arbitration (FAI, ICC, SCC) and Finnish court litigation. We handle the dispute from demand letter through final resolution, with a focus on practical outcomes over procedural theater.

Data Protection & Tech

GDPR compliance, data processing agreements, SaaS contracts, and technology licensing. We understand both the technology and the regulation — because our associate used to build the products.

Local Counsel Coordination

When your matter spans multiple countries, we act as your single point of contact. We manage the foreign lawyers, synthesize the advice, and deliver one coherent answer — not five invoices with five different conclusions.
